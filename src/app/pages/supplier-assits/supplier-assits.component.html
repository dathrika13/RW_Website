Supplier Sustainability Portal

The Redwoods AI Supplier Sustainability Platform: Streamlining Supply Chain Sustainability

The platform tackles key supply chain sustainability challenges:

• Scope 3 emissions data tracking and calculations

• Product life cycle assessments

• Materiality assessments

Key Features

✔Engagement: Our Supplier Sustainability Platform brings all suppliers into one software, simplifying supplier engagement, data collection, and product-level emissions tracking.

✔Emission Data Gathering: Suppliers can collect data from multiple sources and formats using Redwoods AI Services and data integrations.

✔Conversion and Calculation: Inbuilt calculators and conversion tools, based on industry-standard GHG guidelines, simplify carbon emissions accounting.

✔Reporting: Configurable reporting tools align with multiple frameworks, enabling suppliers to report on emissions and ESG data.

✔Summary Dashboard: Aggregate assessments, responses, and insights with training and tools for suppliers to collect scope data, develop sustainability projects, and set reduction targets.
