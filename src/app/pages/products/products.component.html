The SMART Process

Our platform utilizes the SMART process to deliver end-to-end sustainability functionality:

Streamline: Integrate existing data sources from industry-leading systems.

Measure: Collect and track GHG emissions, environmental, social, and governance data.

Accelerate: Set goals, decarbonization targets, and plan strategies using AI-powered predictions and project management tools.

Report: Generate seamless and auditable climate and sustainability reports.

Transform: Drive transformational change by engaging suppliers, partners, and employees to create sustainable products and services.
