Sustainability Solutions for Enterprises

Across the globe, enterprises are facing a new business landscape.  Aggregating, reporting and taking action on GHG emissions, water, waste and other sustainability, social and governance-related KPIs is no longer a “nice to have" it is a "must have".  Governments are requiring it, investors and consumers want it, and the cost of inaction is great including financial penalties, litigation and reputational damage.

Redwoods.ais AI-powered SaaS solution is designed to provide an end-to-end solution that allows a company to accelerate and focus their sustainability efforts powered by next-gen technologies.   Using a SMART process, the platform offers:
  • Out-of-the-box data integrations
  • GHG emissions (scopes 1, 2, 3)
  • Water and waste management
  • Social and Governance data aggregation and tracking
  • Reporting in compliance with multiple frameworks and jurisdictions
  • AI-powered insights and recommendations for target setting
  • Project management support for decarbonization and risk management
Redwoods.ai is excited to feature a breakthrough AI-powered Supplier Sustainability Platform for engagement, streamlined data collection and change management.

ESG Software Platform for Suppliers

As the climate and sustainability regulatory environment for large companies develops, the global suppliers that make up the upstream and downstream value chains of these companies are also being called on to be part of the sustainability transformation.  Whether this is reporting their GHG emissions, use of natural resources, workforce practices or reporting on sector-specific KPIs, current enterprise solutions don’t provide suppliers with the kind of tech-enabled support to meet these increasing demands.

Redwoods.ais Supplier Sustainability Platform is a breakthrough solution that puts the supplier front and center, leveraging AI to ease the data gathering burden on suppliers and unlock engagement and impact.  A customized supplier portal is a one-stop shop for environmental, social and governance data gathering, calculations and reporting. An AI-powered mobile app enables real-time communication and collaboration for data collection and timely reporting for compliance.

ESG Software Platform for Investors

The global investment landscape is increasingly complex and the stakes are high. Portfolio companies must comply with climate and ESG disclosure mandates and greenwashing lawsuits, financial penalties and reputational risks are on the rise. Investors need granular data and insights into the ESG performance of companies so they can allocate and manage their resources most effectively.

Key Features:
  • ESG Ratings/Scoring
  • Industry/Sector Benchmarking
  • Dashboards for Environmental, Social, Governance
  • Automated ESG Data Gathering and Verification
  • Automated Reporting for Multiple Regulations
  • AI-Powered Actionable Insights & Alerts
  • AI-Powered Insights to Reduce Emissions, Cost and Supply Chain Risks
  • Advanced Analytics of Customized KPIs
  • Portfolio Monitoring of Emissions Data and Supplier Performance
  • Streamlined Integrations with other systems
  • Risk Assessment & Management across the Portfolio Companies
