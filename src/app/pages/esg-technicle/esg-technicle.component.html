Sustainability Technical Manager (Hybrid – Pleasanton, CA)  

About Redwoods.ai

Redwoods.ai is on a mission to empower every organization to do better for the planet. The Redwoods AI Copilot sustainability platform is designed that allows enterprises and their suppliers to gather data, plan, report and take action that drives transformative climate and sustainability change.

Job Description

We're seeking an experienced and dynamic Sustainability Technical Manager to join our team. The successful candidate will develop strategic partnerships and engage in the execution and delivery of our software solution to clients.

Key Responsibilities: 

Project Management and Customer Success  

  • Lead product deployment projects from initial scoping to post-implementation support.

  • Engage with customers to co-design project plans, communicate project progress, and problem-solve with clients.

Partnership Development  

  • Identify and engage potential channel partners, establish partner recruitment and onboarding processes, and build strong relationships with channel partners.

Sales and Marketing Support 

  • Support sales and management teams with product demos and joint workshops.

  • Represent the company at industry events and participate in thought leadership initiatives.

Qualifications: 

  • Education: Bachelor's degree in Environmental Studies, Business Administration, Computer Science, or related field. Advanced degree a plus.

  • Expertise: Expertise in carbon accounting and sustainability frameworks and datasets, including compliance with voluntary and mandatory disclosure regimes.

  • Experience: 2+ years of experience in project management, customer success, and account management.

  • Skills: Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a proven ability to manage relationships, prioritize tasks, and drive results in a fast-paced environment.

Benefits:  

  • Competitive salary and benefits package

  • Opportunities for professional development and growth

  • Collaborative and inclusive company culture

  • Meaningful work contributing to positive social and environmental impact
