Key Takeaways from GreenBiz Circularity24 Conference in Chicago

Updated: May 27, 2023

This year, Redwoods.ai had the pleasure of attending GreenBiz’s Circularity24 Conference in Chicago, Illinois, from May 22–24.

This was the fifth annual meeting of the GreenBiz Circularity Conference, and Redwoods.ai was represented by our ESG Technical Manager, Ben Fernando. Ben attended many wonderful presentations and panel discussions and had the opportunity to engage in interesting conversations about what the future of circularity looks like.

From Ben’s time at Circularity24, here are his four takeaways:

  • Policy is and will be one of the biggest drivers of circularity - The policy landscape is growing with exciting new developments in the US and EU that facilitate waste reduction, reuse, and sustainable procurement practices.
  • Collaboration is key - The circular transition requires radical collaboration across nations, sectors, and company functions. Every stakeholder, from policymakers to consumers, is important in this effort.
  • Data, as always, is King - Measuring the impact of circularity and driving business decisions requires robust data collection and analysis. Tools such as lifecycle assessments (LCA) and product carbon footprints (PCF) are essential.
  • The Opportunities for AI are Growing - AI and GenAI tools can significantly assist companies in measuring, managing, and strategizing their circular economy efforts.
